What is history?

The study of history has received many extended and authoritative analyses and definitions. The aim of this site is different: to assemble a very large corpus of definitions of the subject of history and its study by a wide range of people, and to make this data publicly available for computational analysis and re-representation.

Instructions: Simply take a stab at defining history as you understand it at the moment. Please don't spent too much time. Ten minutes should be sufficient. It is also okay to cite another author who you feel expresses things clearly.
